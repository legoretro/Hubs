📖 Resources and Attribution

Information filled in by student.

Primary Reference

Blood Group Antigen FactsBook (3rd ed.)

Reid ME, Lomas-Francis C, Olsson ML. Academic Press / Elsevier, 2012. ISBN 978-0-12-415849-8. Primary source for antigen frequencies, enzyme effects, antibody characteristics, and membrane topology used in this tool.

Textbook

Essentials of Blood Banking and Transfusion Medicine (3rd ed.)

Pilli GS, Pathak S, Nanjannavar SB. CBS Publishers, 2025. Used for system overviews, crossmatch procedures, and clinical context.

Textbook

Essentials of Blood Banking: A Handbook for Students (2nd ed.)

Mehdi SR. Jaypee Brothers Medical Publishers, 2013. Supplementary antibody and system descriptions.

Course Slides

OIT MLS -- Antibody Screen and Crossmatch

Oregon Institute of Technology, Medical Laboratory Science Program. Pre-Transfusion Testing lecture slides covering antibody screen, antigram interpretation, crossmatch phases (IS, 37, AHG, CC), and blood donor selection. Used as primary course reference for the crossmatch simulation design.

Reference Standard

ISBT Blood Group Terminology

International Society of Blood Transfusion Working Party on Red Cell Immunogenetics. System numbers and official antigen nomenclature follow ISBT guidelines as presented in the FactsBook (2012).

Clinical Standard

AABB Technical Manual (current edition)

American Association of Blood Banks. The authoritative clinical reference for blood bank practice. Always consult current edition for clinical protocols.
